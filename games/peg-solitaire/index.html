Peg Solitaire

Jump one peg over another into an empty socket. Leave a single marble in the center.

Pegs: 32 Moves: 0 Select a peg, then a socket two spaces away.
Mouse or touch: choose a marble, then jump over an adjacent marble into an empty socket.
